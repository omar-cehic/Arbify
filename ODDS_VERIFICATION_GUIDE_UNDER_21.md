## üö® **Red Flags (Likely Fake Opportunities):**

### **Suspicious Patterns:**
1. **Unknown/Generic Bookmakers:**
   - "Unknown" bookmaker
   - "Generic" bookmaker  
   - "Test" bookmaker
   - Any bookmaker you've never heard of

2. **Unrealistic Odds:**
   - Consistent +100 odds from one bookmaker
   - Profit margins >15% (extremely rare)
   - Odds that seem "too good to be true"

3. **Market Mismatches:**
   - Different line values being compared
   - Different players or teams
   - Different market types (e.g., hits vs total bases)
